DR. Ramoni O. Lasisi, Ph.D.

ASSISTANT PROFESSOR

DR. Ramoni Lasisi

Ph.D. - Utah State University

Dr. Ramoni Lasisi is an assistant professor in the Computer Science Department at VMI.

More info

Dr. Lasisi holds the IEEE Senior Membership grade. Only 7% of IEEE's approximately 431,000 members worldwide hold this grade, which requires extensive experience, and reflects professional maturity and documented achievements of significance. He is also a member of ACM and SIAM. research interests include: • Issues at the intersection of computer science (artificial intelligence, multiagent  systems, and algorithms) and economics (game theory and mechanism design). These include negotiation and decision-making in multiagent systems involving voting theory, social choice domain, and cooperative game theory.• Data Mining, Data Science, and Data Analysis.
• Bioinformatics and computational biology, including RNA secondary structures predictions.SIAM
